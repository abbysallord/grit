Overview

How GRIT Works

Two ways to reach Finale — stack miles through contests and bonuses, or bypass rankings entirely with a standout achievement.

01
Skill Contests
  • Attempt any Skill Level in GRIT contests
  • Gold = full miles · Silver = 90% miles
  • Best score across all attempts counts
  • Silver/Gold unlocks next level
  • Retry after 5 days minimum gap
02
Track Badges
  • Complete required skills per badge tier
  • Pass mandatory Interview Round
  • Sequential — Novice before Specialist
  • Bonus miles on top of skill miles (TBA)
03
External Bonuses
  • Codeforces, LeetCode, CodeChef ratings
  • ISRO Robotics, SIH, NIAT Makers
  • All milestones are cumulative
  • Submit proof via GRIT Dashboard
04
Direct Ticket
  • One achievement = skip straight to Finale
  • ACM ICPC · GSoC · Top Accelerators
  • Research papers · Content creation
  • Window: Feb 2026 – Feb 2027

Critical Rules

Don't Get Tripped Up

Nine rules you must internalize. Missing any one can cost you miles or a badge.

1
5-Day Retry Gap
Minimum 5 days between attempts of the same Skill Level. Plan your week — don't waste an attempt unprepared.
2
Best Score Always Wins
Highest score across all attempts counts. You're never penalized for retrying — always worth one more shot.
3
Silver/Gold Unlocks Next Level
"Try Again" does NOT unlock L2. Earn at least Silver to progress. Aim Gold every single time.
4
Badge Interview is Mandatory
Completing skill levels isn't enough. You must pass the Interview Round to officially earn the badge and claim bonus miles.
5
Proof Required for Bonuses
CP bonuses are NOT auto-credited. Submit valid proof via GRIT Dashboard. Screenshot everything now, submit later.
6
Ticket is Non-Transferable
Awarded to a specific individual only. Max 2 co-founders per startup under Entrepreneurship path.
7
NIAT Can Revise Anything
Skills, scoring, miles, attempt rules, track structures — all revisable at any time. Monitor official channels.
8
Locked Skills = Future Miles
DS&ML (L1–L4), Data Intelligence, Quant Finance, Human Skills are all locked. Be the first through the door when they open.
9
Team Bonuses = Individual Miles
For ISRO, SIH, NIAT Makers — every eligible team member gets the full bonus miles individually. Team up strategically.
